TESTIMONIAL

The launch of the Civil Innovation and Technology Cell (CITC) has been a transformative experience for our department. By fostering collaboration between traditional civil engineering expertise and the exciting world of AI, ML, and digital integration, the CITC is truly pushing the boundaries of what's possible. Their three specialized teams, each with unique strengths, ensure a holistic approach to every project. The CITC doesn't just develop innovative solutions - they empower individuals within the department to grow their skillsets and become leaders in this dynamic field. I wholeheartedly recommend the CITC to anyone passionate about advancing civil engineering through the power of technology.

Prof. Rajib Saha Dean Academic, NIT Agartala

The CITC initiative has been a transformative opportunity for our department. It has provided our students with hands-on exposure to industry practices, offering insights that go far beyond textbooks. By directly engaging with real-world challenges, our students are not only learning technical skills but also developing problem-solving, teamwork, and adaptability—qualities essential for thriving in the competitive landscape of modern engineering. This program truly strengthens the bridge between academic learning and practical application, ensuring that our future engineers are equipped to meet the demands of the industry with confidence and competence.

Prof. Deb Dulal Tripura HOD Civil Engineering Department

The addition of the CITC program has significantly transformed our department's approach to education. By integrating real-world industry challenges with academic learning, students are given a unique opportunity to apply their theoretical knowledge in practical settings. As a result, our graduates are not only well-prepared academically but also equipped with the confidence and skills to excel in their professional careers, bridging the gap between classroom theory and industry practice.

Dr. Dipankar Sarkar Assistant Professor
