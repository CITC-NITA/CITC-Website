Webinar Recap: UPSC ESE AIR 20 Topper “Kishan Kumar” Shares His Secrets to Success

Welcome to the official blog of the exclusive webinar session with UPSC ESE AIR 20 Topper Kishan Kumar!


In an inspiring and insightful webinar conducted through a joint collaboration between CITC and CES, UPSC ESE topper Kishan Kumar, who secured All India Rank 20, shared his preparation strategies, personal experiences, and key lessons for all aspiring candidates.


Held online with a massive turnout of enthusiastic participants, the session transformed into more than just a knowledge-sharing event—it became a source of motivation and clarity for those preparing for one of India’s most prestigious engineering services exams.


Who is Kishan Kumar?

Kishan Kumar is a shining example of perseverance, smart strategy, and dedication. With an incredible feat of securing AIR 20 in ESE and AIR 244 in GATE, he has done his Graduation in Civil Engineering from NIT Silchar now he has become a role model for thousands of aspirants who dream of serving the country through engineering excellence.


Key Takeaways from the Webinar

  • 1. Foundation is Everything:

    Kishan emphasized building strong conceptual clarity in core subjects before diving into question practice. Understanding fundamentals was highlighted as the real key to success.

  • 2. The Role of Consistency:

    Consistency over intensity—Kishan recommended regular, disciplined study schedules and encouraged aspirants to seek mentorship instead of preparing in isolation.

  • 3. Smart Work Over Hard Work:

    Solving previous year papers, analyzing trends, and focusing on high-yield topics were his top strategies. He also shared mock test planning and effective revision methods.

  • 4. Mental Health & Motivation:

    Stress and self-doubt are part of the journey. Kishan talked openly about emotional resilience and emphasized having a strong support system of friends, mentors, and family.


Interactive Q&A Session

The session concluded with a vibrant Q&A, where Kishan answered queries ranging from time management to last-month hacks. His honest and detailed responses helped clear many doubts and boosted morale.


Final Words

The webinar was more than an event—it was a heartfelt interaction between a successful candidate and the aspirants following his footsteps. The collaboration between CITC and CES made it possible to bring such an enriching experience to the student community.


Stay Inspired!

Stay connected for more such knowledge-driven events, mentorship sessions, and opportunities that help you unlock your true potential. As Kishan Kumar proved, the journey is tough—but absolutely achievable.
