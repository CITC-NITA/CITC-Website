Arjuna 1.0: North East Tech Summit 2024!

Welcome to the official blog of the Arjuna 1.0: North East Tech Summit !


The stage was set, and the countdown concluded with the grand success of Arjuna 1.0—the first-ever tech summit hosted by the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Agartala, in collaboration with the Civil Innovation and Technology Cell (CITC). This groundbreaking initiative proved to be a beacon of innovation and collaboration, bringing together the brightest minds from across North East India and beyond.

This Hackathon contained total 3 problem statement, they are as follows :-


  • Mental Well-Being Surveillance, Tracking, and Assessment System

    Mental health is crucial, especially for children. We're seeking a solution that allows continuous monitoring and assessment of children's mental well-being, providing personalized recommendations to ensure a supportive environment for growth. Your project could pave the way for a healthier, more mindful future.

  • Advancing Landslide Detection & Warning System

    Imagine saving lives with just a click! We're calling on innovators to develop cutting-edge systems using AI and IoT that can predict landslides, providing timely warnings to those at risk. Your solution could revolutionize disaster management, making our world a safer place.

  • Groundwater Recharge

    Water scarcity is one of the most critical issues of our time. This problem statement invites you to devise sustainable strategies for recharging groundwater, ensuring that our precious water resources are available for generations to come. Your solution could make a significant difference in the fight against water depletion.

Accomplishment of Event

  • Winner Selections

    One team from each problem statement considered to be winners.They are as follows

    • Team MindtraqFrom Problem statement 01:Mental Well-Being Surveillance, Tracking, and Assessment System
    • Team Need for GitFrom Problem statement 02:Advancing Landslide Detection & Warning System
    • Team Agro TitansFrom Problem statement 03:Groundwater Recharge

  • Each wining team has given a price of 20,000 Rs., a momento and a certificate

  • Our journey at Arjuna 1.0 was filled with panel discussions, riveting demos, and inspiring keynote addresses. The participants showcased incredible tech solutions, tackling real-world challenges across domains like mental health assessment, landslide detection and more.

  • The experience was nothing short of transformative, bringing together a vibrant community of innovators from diverse backgrounds, united by a shared passion for problem-solving. We are immensely proud to have witnessed the ingenuity and resilience of these future changemakers.
