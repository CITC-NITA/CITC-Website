From Aayam to SIH Glory: Our Journey at the Smart India Hackathon 2023

The Civil Innovation & Technology Cell is thrilled to share our exhilarating adventure at the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2023! This wasn't just a competition; it was a rollercoaster ride of innovation, teamwork, and pushing our tech skills to the limit.


Two Teams, One Dream: Reaching the SIH Grand Finale

We're incredibly proud to announce that not one, but two of our teams made it to the prestigious SIH Grand Finale! Let's meet the masterminds behind these remarkable achievements:


Team Bitwise Decoy (Software Edition):

  • The Dream Team:

    Shivam Kumar , Roma Ghosh, Alisha Ansari, Hemant Kumar, Shivam Kumar and Manish Biswas .

  • The Challenge:

    Developing an innovative e-waste facility locator.

  • The Journey:

    Out of a staggering 500 ideas at the SIH Internal Hackathon on our campus, theirs rocketed to the top 5, securing them a spot at the Grand Finale!

  • The Grand Finale:

    The legendary P.R. Pote Patil College, Amravati, became their battleground for a grueling 36-hour coding marathon. Through brainstorming sessions, laughter, and moments of intense focus, they persevered.

  • The Result:

    Team Bitwise Decoy emerged victorious, snagging the prestigious Runner-Up title at the nodal center!

Team Pavaan (Hardware Edition):

  • The Innovation Squad:

    Sreyashi Adhikary, Krisanu Nath, Udayan Banik, Bishmoy Kumar Das, Nipobithi Bagchi, and Bishudhananda Adhikari.

  • The Challenge:

    "Development of Small Scale Wind Energy Device."

  • The Feat:

    From a pool of 321 submissions across India, their idea landed them in the top 5! This accomplishment is even more impressive considering the massive participation of over 1,660,000 students.

  • The Road to Glory:

    Qualifying in the SIH Internal Hackathon was just the beginning. Reaching the finals is a testament to their unwavering teamwork and collaborative spirit.

  • The Gratitude:

    A special thanks goes out to Sucheta Kolekar, David Saldanha, Dr. Cyril Joseph, and the entire team at MAHE Manipal Institute of Technology for their unwavering support and guidance throughout the process.

Beyond the Competition: A Celebration of Innovation

The SIH Grand Finale was more than just a competition; it was a vibrant hub where ideas blossomed, and budding tech talents like yourselves had the opportunity to shine. We're incredibly grateful to SIH for providing a platform that allows us to showcase our skills and transform dreams into reality.
