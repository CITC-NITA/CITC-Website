CIVITAS : A Grand Orientation Event by CITC, NIT Agartala

The Civil Innovation and Technology Club (CITC) of NIT Agartala successfully hosted its orientation event, CIVITAS, an engaging session designed exclusively for first-year students. Held with great enthusiasm, the event provided freshers with a deep insight into the club’s activities, opportunities, and its pivotal role in shaping young civil engineers.


Event Overview


CIVITAS was meticulously planned to introduce the first-year students to the world of civil engineering beyond textbooks. The session highlighted past achievements of the club, ongoing projects, and the roadmap for upcoming workshops, including those to be conducted during AAYAM, the cultural fest of NIT Agartala.

Students also got introduced to Arjuna 2.0, an upcoming hackathon designed to showcase innovative solutions and problem-solving skills.


Key Highlights



  • 1. Introduction to CITC

    • A brief history and the vision of the club.
    • CITC’s role in fostering innovation and practical skills in civil engineering.


  • 2. Placement and Project Opportunities

    • Guidance on future placements and internships.
    • Hands-on projects with real-world impact.
    • Showcasing past successful student-led initiatives.


  • 3. Upcoming Workshops and Events

    • Announcements on technical sessions and workshops.
    • Preview of workshops during AAYAM.
    • Introduction to Arjuna 2.0 Hackathon.


  • 4. Interactive Sessions

    • Open discussions with seniors and alumni.
    • Career path guidance and doubt-clearing sessions.
    • First-hand experiences shared by club members.



Audience Experience & Engagement


The event witnessed an overwhelming response. Freshers actively participated, engaged in discussions, and connected with seniors. Many expressed excitement to join the club’s initiatives, including workshops, placement sessions, and future projects.


Impact & Takeaways


CIVITAS wasn’t just an orientation—it was an inspiration. Students walked away with a deeper understanding of the scope and potential of civil engineering through CITC. The event laid the foundation for a community of young innovators ready to explore, create, and lead.


Conclusion & Call to Action


With CIVITAS, CITC once again proved its commitment to nurturing young talent. If you missed it, don't worry—this is just the beginning. Stay tuned for exciting events, engaging workshops, and groundbreaking projects. Follow CITC on social media and be a part of our journey to revolutionize civil engineering!
