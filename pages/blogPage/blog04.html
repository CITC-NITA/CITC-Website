Bitwise Decoy: Unveiling a Multifaceted Solution for E-Waste Management

The Civil Innovation & Technology Cell is thrilled to introduce Team Bitwise Decoy and their remarkable project, a multifaceted solution for tackling the ever-growing challenge of e-waste management.


A Solution with Layers: Recovering Value, Rewarding Users, and Fostering Collaboration

Team Bitwise Decoy's project goes beyond simply locating e-waste collection points. It's a comprehensive platform designed to incentivize responsible e-waste disposal and streamline the recycling process. Here's a breakdown of their ingenious solution:


  1. AI/ML for Metal Recovery Estimation:

    Imagine a system that can predict the amount of recoverable metal from your e-waste based on its model number! This is achieved through AI and Machine Learning, trained on extensive datasets. Feature engineering and model training ensure accuracy in these estimations.

  2. Educational Pop-Ups:

    While users earn rewards, the system also educates them! Informative pop-ups raise awareness about the harmful components of e-waste, promoting responsible disposal practices

  3. Locating E-Waste Collection Centers:

    Gone are the days of searching endlessly for e-waste collection points. The project leverages a location tracker powered by the Geolocation API to identify the nearest authorized e-waste trader in your vicinity.

  4. Seamless User Experience:

    • 24/7 AI Chatbot:

      Seeking information or assistance? A 24/7 AI-powered chatbot is always available to answer your queries and guide you through the process.

    • 3-Layered Deep Neural Network Model:

      This advanced model forms the backbone of the chatbot, ensuring intelligent and informative interactions.

    • Text-to-Speech Feature:

      Accessibility is key! The project utilizes Google's Text-to-Speech feature to provide audible information for users who prefer audio communication

The Tech Stack Behind the Innovation:

Team Bitwise Decoy utilized a robust tech stack to bring their vision to life:


  • Front-End:

    HTML, CSS, Tailwind, JavaScript

  • Geolocation and Mapping:

    OpenStreetMap API (Leaflet)

  • Database Operations:

    Mongoose API, MongoDB, Node.js, Handlebars.js

  • Server-Side Logic:

    JavaScript Framework (Express.js)

  • AI/ML Model Training:

    Python, JSON files (datasets)

  • Chatbot Development:

    Flask, Keras Sequential API, TensorFlow

  • Text-to-Speech:

    Google Text-to-Speech API

Conclusion: A Brighter Future for E-Waste Management

Team Bitwise Decoy's project presents a compelling vision for a more responsible and efficient approach to e-waste management. By combining AI/ML, user incentives, and collaboration, the project offers a promising solution for a global challenge.
