AV-TURBOTIDE: Sailing Towards Cleaner Waters

The Civil Innovation & Technology Cell is on a mission! Today, we're excited to unveil AV-TURBOTIDE, our innovative project tackling the global crisis of water pollution.This project took the center stage at 34th Tripura Industries and Commerce Fair, 2024.


A Two-Pronged Attack: Data-Driven Monitoring and Targeted Cleanup

AV-TURBOTIDE utilizes a dynamic duo to combat water pollution:


  • Module 1: The Sensor Boat (Data Acquisition):

    Imagine a valiant eco-warrior disguised as a sleek boat. This isn't your average vessel; it's a real-time water quality detective equipped with a powerhouse of sensors:

    • pH Sensor:

      Monitors water acidity/alkalinity, a crucial factor for maintaining a healthy aquatic ecosystem.

    • Turbidity Sensor:

      Detects the presence of suspended particles, indicating potential pollution or erosion.

    • Dissolved Oxygen Sensor:

      Measures oxygen levels, vital for fish survival and microbial activity.

    • TDS Sensor:

      Quantifies total dissolved solids, highlighting potential contaminants.

    • Temperature Sensor:

      Tracks water temperature, which impacts oxygen solubility and pollutant behavior.

This data-gathering marvel is powered by an Arduino Mega microcontroller and transmits sensor data wirelessly. The information feeds into a user-friendly website accessible from any device. Additionally, a convenient QR code displayed on the boat allows anyone to scan and instantly view the water quality status.


  • Module 2: The Cleanup Boat (Pollution Fighter):

    While Module 1 gathers vital intel, Module 2 takes decisive action. This secondary boat springs into action when predefined pollution thresholds, detected by Module 1, are reached. Depending on the specific contaminants identified, targeted cleanup strategies are deployed. Here are some potential methods:

    • Bioremediation:

      Nature's superheroes! Utilizing bacteria or algae to naturally break down pollutants.

    • Sorption

      Employing absorbent materials like charcoal or zeolites to trap contaminants.

    • Mechanical Filtration:

      Removing suspended particles and debris through physical filtration systems.

Phase 2: The Power of AI and ML

AV-TURBOTIDE doesn't stop there! Phase 2 takes the project to new heights by harnessing the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). Imagine these robots autonomously navigating polluted waters, optimizing data collection and cleanup strategies in real-time. Here's a glimpse into the exciting future:


  • Automated Operations:

    AI algorithms can analyze sensor data and independently control both boats, maximizing efficiency and responsiveness.

  • Targeted Cleanup:

    Using data from Module 1, ML can precisely deploy Module 2 to areas requiring the most urgent attention.

  • Pollution Source Identification:

    By integrating Google Maps and AI, the system can analyze water quality data to identify potential pollution sources like nearby industries, enabling targeted interventions.

Beyond Technology: Building a Community of Champions

AV-TURBOTIDE is more than just cutting-edge technology. It's about fostering community engagement and raising awareness.


  • Project Website (https://av-turbotide.netlify.app/): A Hub for Action:

    The website serves as a central platform for:

    • Sharing data with stakeholders.

    • Educating the public about water pollution and its impact.

    • Inspiring further action and collaboration.

  • Community Involvement:

    Local communities become active participants in:

    • Data collection efforts.

    • Boat maintenance.

    • Designing innovative cleanup solutions.

Conclusion: A Brighter Future for Our Waterways

AV-TURBOTIDE is a beacon of hope in the fight for clean water. Combining real-time data collection, targeted cleanup, and AI-powered automation, this project has the potential to revolutionize our approach to water pollution. By embracing technology, engaging communities, and fostering environmental awareness, AV-TURBOTIDE can pave the way for a cleaner future, one wave at a time.
